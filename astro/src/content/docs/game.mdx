## System Requirements

<div style="margin: 2rem 0; padding: 1.5rem; background: rgba(99, 102, 241, 0.05); border-radius: 8px; border-left: 4px solid #6366f1;">

### Recommended Specifications

- **Browser**: Latest version of Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari
- **WebGL**: WebGL 2.0 support required
- **RAM**: 4GB or more recommended
- **Internet**: Stable broadband connection for initial load
- **Display**: 1280x720 resolution or higher

### Performance Notes

For the best experience, close unnecessary browser tabs and ensure hardware acceleration is enabled in your browser settings.

</div>
